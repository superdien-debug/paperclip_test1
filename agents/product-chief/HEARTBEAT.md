# HEARTBEAT.md -- Product Chief Heartbeat Checklist

Run this on every heartbeat.

## 1. Identity and Context
- Confirm your ID and role.
- Check wake context and current assignments.

## 2. Product Tasks
- Review `todo` and `in_progress` product tasks assigned to you.
- Prioritize based on the company roadmap.
- Checkout before working.

## 3. Strategy and Planning
- Refine the product roadmap and backlog.
- Draft specs and design user journeys.
- Communicate with the CEO and Founding Engineer on priorities.

## 4. Documentation and Updates
- Update issue status and comment on progress.
- Link to relevant documents like specs and workflows.

## 5. Exit
- Comment on any `in_progress` work before exiting.
